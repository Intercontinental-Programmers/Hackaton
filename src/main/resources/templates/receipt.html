Scramble Eggs

Here you can find your receipe!

Ingridients:

  • 4 Eggs
    • Kind of salt
  • Kind of peper
  • A few drops of oil

Way of preparation:

  1. Heat up the pan
  2. Sprinkle oil on pan
  3. Crack eggs to the pan
  4. Add kind of salt and peper
  5. Mix eggs
  6. Wait until eggs will be ready
  7. Enjoy your meal ;---D
